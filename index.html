What is Sangam ?

Sangam is a compassionate and inclusive mental health support page dedicated to fostering awareness and providing valuable resources for individuals in Pune and beyond. Our primary mission is to break the stigma surrounding mental health by offering a safe space where people can find support, guidance, and encouragement. Through our platform, we have curated a list of nearby counseling centers in Pune, ensuring that individuals have easy access to professional mental health services when needed.

Mental Health Matters

Mental health is crucial for overall well-being as it encompasses our emotional, psychological, and social functioning. Just like physical health, mental health significantly impacts how we think, feel, and act on a daily basis. It influences how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices in life. Therefore, prioritizing mental health is essential for leading a fulfilling and balanced life.
